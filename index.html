About Me

Electronics have fascinated me from childhood; the way such tiny elements could drive complex devices always amazed me. Most of the revolutionary gadgets we see today have electronics at their heart. As a child, I always loved to open the devices available and peek inside; initially, it seemed magical but slowly. As I started understanding, my interest in electronics grew further. I still remember when I first restored one of my non-functioning toys, the joy this gave me back then is beyond words.

Being at one of India's prestigious academic institutions, I get an opportunity to engage with our nation's brightest minds, which helps me broaden my horizons and push me to strive for excellence. I hope to continue such engagement as, in my opinion, the best way to learn or design something novel is to discuss, teach and engage with the community.
